This has been a full week. The biggest thing is the site itself — I finally rebuilt `leochilds.github.io` in Astro, replacing the old Jekyll setup that had been gathering dust. The new version has a proper projects index and this blog, which didn't exist before. It also gave me an excuse to do a proper audit of my public repos and tidy up the descriptions.

The Kyri & Cou cryptography companion site also saw good progress. It's sitting at the top of my projects page now, which feels right — it's the thing I'm most actively building at the moment. The goal is an interactive browser-based companion to the YouTube course, so you can explore ciphers and break them as you go through each lesson.

Beyond those two: `fasting-app`, `property-game`, `library`, and the `osrs-stale-bank-plugin` all got pushes this week. `LionPDF` had some work earlier in the week too. I also published `malwi-samples` — a dataset repo for training the malwi AI malware scanner. It's been a missing piece for a while and good to have it out in the open.

Not a bad week to be starting a blog.
